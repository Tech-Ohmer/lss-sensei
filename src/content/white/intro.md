# Welcome to Six Sigma: The Power of ROI

Six Sigma is more than just a certificate; it's a **data-driven revolution**. Developed by Motorola and perfected by GE under Jack Welch, it transformed how the world views quality.

## The Financial Impact (ROI)
Why do companies care? 
- **Motorola**: Saved over **$16 Billion** in its first decade.
- **GE**: Added **$12 Billion** to their bottom line through Six Sigma projects.
